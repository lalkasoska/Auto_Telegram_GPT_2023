Idea: A subscription-based meal prep service that utilizes blockchain technology to ensure freshness and traceability of ingredients. 

Problem: Consumers often struggle to trust whether the food they purchase is truly fresh and healthy, leading to food waste and health concerns. 

Target Audience: Health-conscious consumers who prioritize transparency and sustainability in their food choices. 

Revenue Model: Recurring subscription fees and additional revenue from sales of premium ingredients and add-ons.

Prompt: Blockchain Food.
