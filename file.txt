Idea: A meal delivery service that provides healthy, pre-made meals using locally-sourced, organic ingredients. 
Problem: Many people struggle with finding the time to cook healthy meals for themselves due to busy schedules and lack of knowledge about nutrition. 
Target Audience: Busy professionals, health-conscious individuals, and families looking for an easy and affordable way to eat healthy. 
Revenue Model: Subscription-based service with tiered pricing based on the number of meals ordered. 

Prompt: Healthy Meal
